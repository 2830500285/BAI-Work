# EBAI slide 11 layout design

## Communication job

By the end of slide 11, hackathon judges should immediately understand that EBAI is BAI Work's parent product capability layer and that Commands, Agents, Skills, Rules, and Hooks are the five capability forms it presents to users.

## Options considered

1. Parent layer plus five-card capability cluster. Reuse the EBAI card as a full-width parent band and place the five capability cards beneath it. This makes the hierarchy explicit without changing any copy.
2. Left-side EBAI spine plus a 3 + 2 capability matrix. This creates a stronger architecture silhouette, but gives the five capability forms uneven visual weight.
3. Keep six peer cards and only tighten spacing. This is the smallest change, but continues to imply that EBAI is a peer of Commands, Agents, Skills, Rules, and Hooks.

## Decision

Use option 1. The current content is already accurate; the problem is hierarchy. The revised composition will:

- place EBAI in a full-width parent band directly beneath the title;
- distribute Commands, Agents, Skills, Rules, and Hooks in five equal cards;
- align the two explanatory panels on a shared baseline;
- preserve the concluding product statement, evidence footer, page marker, colors, typography, and all visible copy.

The change is layout-only. It does not restore the older external-asset conversion pipeline and does not modify application code.
